ü§î What Does P-Value Mean?

P-value = "What are the chances this happened by pure luck?"

  • p < 0.05 = Less than 5% chance it's random = üö® REAL PATTERN
  • p > 0.05 = More than 5% chance it's random = ü§∑ COULD BE LUCK

Think of it like this: If you flip a coin 10 times and get 8 heads, is the coin rigged? P-value tells you the odds!
